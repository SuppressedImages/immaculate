Suppressed Images: Picturing Chelsea Manning is a comic-book illustration of a collaboration that took place between artist Heather Dewey-Hagborg and whistleblower Chelsea Manning in 2015. In the collaboration, titled Radical Love, Heather created portraits of Chelsea from DNA that she extracted from Chelsea’s hair clippings and cheek swabs.

Suppressed Images: Picturing Chelsea Manning goes behind the scenes to show details of the work’s process and ends with a speculative twist in which Chelsea Manning’s sentence is commuted, and she is released so that she can visit an exhibition of her portrait in person.
