In 2015, Chelsea Manning and artist Heather Dewey-Hagborg collaborated on an artwork titled Radical Love. In this work, Heather created portraits of Chelsea based on DNA extracted from her hair clippings and cheek swabs.

Suppressed Images is a graphic short story illustrating this collaboration, and how it took place.
